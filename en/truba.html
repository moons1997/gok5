Characteristics of fiberglass reinforcement
Corrosion resistance
The components of the composite have a low reactivity,
therefore do not undergo electrochemical corrosion
and bacterial degradation.
Weather resistance
The range of boundary temperatures (-60 ° С + 80 ° С),
treatment of pipes with a gelcoat protective layer
provides immunity to UV rays.
Low thermal conductivity
Virtually no cold

Does not conduct electricity
